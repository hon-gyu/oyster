# Advanced Math Examples

## Greek Letters

Common symbols: $\alpha$, $\beta$, $\gamma$, $\Delta$, $\pi$, $\sigma$, $\theta$, $\omega$

Capital letters: $\Gamma$, $\Lambda$, $\Sigma$, $\Omega$

## Fractions and Binomials

Nested fraction: $\frac{1}{1 + \frac{1}{2}}$

Binomial coefficient: $\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}$

Complex fraction: $\frac{x^2 + 2x + 1}{x^2 - 1}$

## Summations and Products

Sum of natural numbers:

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

Factorial as a product:

$$
\prod_{i=1}^{n} i = n!
$$

Infinite series:

$$
\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2^n} = 2
$$

## Matrices

A 2x2 matrix:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
$$

A 3x3 identity matrix:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## Calculus

Derivative definition: $\frac{d}{dx} f(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}$

Integral: $\int_a^b f(x) dx$

Partial derivatives: $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$, $\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2}$

## Systems of Equations

$$
\begin{cases}
x + y = 5 \\
2x - y = 1
\end{cases}
$$

## Limits

$$
\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} = 0
$$

$$
\lim_{n \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^n = e
$$
